Cardrews Media is a video production company in North Wales created in 2015 by its senior producer, Robert A-Wood, a talented and self-confessed media nerd, with high aspirations. Rob studied Film and Media Sciences at the prestigious Tribeca Flashpoint College, Chicago.

In the United States Rob took master classes in producing and directing with seasoned veterans of the film industry and was offered remarkable opportunities working with Chicago based actors and producers, creating films and gaining invaluable experience.

On his return from the United States Rob’s dream was to build a dynamic video and audio production company. Equipped with professional knowledge and industry experience, Rob took the plunge and set up Cardrews Media with the specific mission of offering low cost but high end filming to local companies.

Cardrews Media offers a complete production process. As Rob says, “We don’t just turn up and film. We listen to our clients’ business targets before we script, cast and film. Then once the first draft is produced, we have a review period for post-production changes. Ultimately we ensure that our clients are fully satisfied before the film goes live.”
